Next 60 days

Three priorities for the next 60 days.

01

Activate the other 141.

Half the roster hasn't opened a page yet. Department-targeted first-touch emails — one high-relevance grant each — are the highest-leverage move. Start where rosters are large but penetration is low: English, Sociology, Biology.

02

Lean on us for rollout support.

We'll provide whatever helps adoption — a live faculty demo or webinar, department-specific one-pagers, ready-to-send outreach email templates, and office hours for your team. Tell us which formats land best at Cortland.

03

Drive AI assistant adoption.

Only 11 researchers have tried Atom's AI chat — the surface where intent-based matching shines. A short faculty demo plus a digest feature can move it from a handful of users to a habit, and start building the year-one ROI story.
