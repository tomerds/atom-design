05 / Our recommendation

Why we’d start with the Full Package.

  • The proposals module is where Maygan’s work lives
    Pre-award prep, document collection, and tracking what is complete and ready to pull down all sit inside one board, not scattered across email and Google.
  • The red-line review was the standout for you
    It scales the funder-aligned review you can only do for the biggest grants today across all ~30 proposals a year.
  • Discovery alone leaves the throughput problem unsolved
    More opportunities in front of nine labs means more proposals to manage. The Full package handles both the pipeline and the throughput.

06 / A path to a better price

Two ways to lower the price.

Option 1

Multiyear commitment

Lock in a lower annual rate the longer you commit.

  • 3-year term: a reduced annual rate, held flat for the term
  • 5-year term: our lowest annual rate, held flat for the term
Option 2

Case study commitment

Share your results and we lower the rate.

  • Publish a case study with us after a successful 180-day check-in
  • In exchange, we apply a reduced annual rate

Either path can be built straight into the formal quote. Tell us which fits and we’ll structure the discount rather than leave it to negotiate later.
